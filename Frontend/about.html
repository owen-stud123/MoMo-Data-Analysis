About Mobile Payment SMS Insights

Mobile Payment SMS Insights is a powerful, user-friendly dashboard designed to help individuals manage and analyze their mobile money (MoMo) transactions. By processing and visualizing SMS data, we empower users to gain deeper insights into their financial behavior and spending habits.

Our Mission

We aim to simplify financial management. Whether you're tracking expenses, monitoring transfers, or tracking spending, our platform is here to assist you.

Who is it for?

  • Mobile money users who want to monitor their transaction history.
  • Budget-conscious individuals looking for spending insights.
  • Anyone needing a clean, organized view of their MoMo activity.

What We Offer

  • Intuitive Dashboard: Clean, organized layout for quick access to data.
  • Real-time Visualization: Interactive graphs and charts powered by Chart.js.
  • Smart Categorization: Automatically groups messages by transaction type (e.g., utilities, airtime, bank transfers).
  • Secure Data Handling: All information is processed and stored securely.

We believe that managing your mobile finances should be simple, transparent, and insightful. That's what drives us to keep improving this platform for you.
